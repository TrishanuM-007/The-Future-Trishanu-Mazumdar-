In the future, the model of education will be worlds apart from what it is today. In 20 years time, students from all backgrounds and with varying interests will be accessing world-leading teaching solutions that will nurture their skills and give them control over their own education. AI or Artificial Intelligence will play a major role in education. Students would be placed in classes not by age but by competence and potential.

The job genres will be diverse and opportunities will be distributed equally. Not only doctors and engineers, all the professions like artists, dancers, singers, will be equally in demand and there will be progress through all aspects in the country.
